GP Awasari Club

Welcome to the official webpage of GP Awasari Club! We are a vibrant community of students passionate about various activities and interests. Our club is dedicated to fostering creativity, learning, and camaraderie among our members.

About Us

GP Awasari Club was established in 2010 with the aim of providing a platform for students to explore their interests and develop new skills. We organize a wide range of events, workshops, and competitions throughout the year, covering topics such as technology, arts, sports, and social causes.

Our Activities

  • Tech Talks and Coding Workshops
  • Art and Craft Sessions
  • Sports Tournaments
  • Community Service Projects
  • Cultural Events and Festivals
